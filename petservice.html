FAQ For Cat Hotel

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What services does MeowTel provide?

MeowTel provides cat hotel boarding, professional grooming, daily feeding, playtime, and a safe, comfortable environment for your beloved cats.

2. Are vaccinations required?

Yes. Cats should have up-to-date vaccinations before staying at our hotel to ensure the safety of all pets.

3. Can I bring my cat's own food?

Yes. Owners are welcome to provide their cat's regular food and any special dietary instructions.

4. How many cats can stay in one room?

Capacity depends on the room type. Please refer to each room's details for the maximum number of cats allowed.

5. Do you provide grooming services?

Yes. We offer bathing, trimming, nail clipping, ear cleaning, and additional grooming services.

6. How can I make a booking?

Simply complete the booking form above or click any Book Now button to contact us via WhatsApp.

7. Can I visit the hotel before booking?

Yes. You may arrange an appointment to visit our cat hotel before making your reservation.
