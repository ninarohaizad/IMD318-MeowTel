About Us

Introduction of MeowTel

Background Organisation

MeowTel was born from a simple wish to make sure every cat receives the best possible care while their owners are away. We understand just how precious your little furball is to your family and that trust is something we never take lightly. Starting out as a small cat hotel in Cyberjaya, Selangor. MeowTel has since grown into a trusted boarding destination for cat owners across Malaysia. Every room we offer is purpose built for your cat's comfort and safety, backed by a team that genuinely loves what they do.

From round the clock care to gentle grooming, our goal has always stayed the same to treat every cat that stays with us like an honoured guest and to give owners complete peace of mind while they're away.
